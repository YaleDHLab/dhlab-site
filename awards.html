The DHLab welcomes graduate students to complete our Digital Humanities Certificate. After attending our DH Foundations workshop, certificate students will consult with DHLab staff to choose a total of six experiences (satisfied through a personalized combination of coursework, training, and/or project work) in order to demonstrate digital humanities competencies across four possible themes.

Graduate students are invited to apply for our digital humanities mini-grants, which will provide space, community, mentorship, and funding for graduate students building digital projects that ask humanistic questions.
